About Founders

At Founders, we specialize in consulting our clients to prosper their developments through strategic business planning and marketing. Since our establishment in 2019, we have made it our top priority to deeply understand our clients' needs and preferences and specialize in sales and marketing. From its inception as a subsidiary of Hyde Park Developments, Founders has adopted a distinct business model that has enabled us to attain significant accomplishments within a relatively brief span, resulting in unparalleled success. Our alliance with El Ahly Sabbour was instrumental in developing the marketing and sales strategy for two exceptional projects - Gardenia and Wesal. These projects, owned by the National Bank of Egypt and Banque Misr respectively, were a testament to our commitment to delivering unparalleled marketing services that guarantee convenience and satisfaction for our clients.

We are confident in our ability to provide top-notch solutions that exceed expectations and achieve outstanding Results. In addition, we are proud to announce that all our projects are fully finished and ready to move in. We offer our clients the convenience of a 10% down payment and flexible installment plans of up to 10 years. This means that you can secure your new home with ease and enjoy the flexibility of affordable payments over an extended period. We are dedicated to providing B2B solutions that enable our clients to achieve their sales goals. Join us today and let us help you create a strategy that will take your business to the next level.
