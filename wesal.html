About Project

Discover Wesal, A fully integrated city situated directly on Suez Road in East Cairo featuring fully finished homesready for immediate occupancy. Spanning an expansive 800 Acres with over 1.2 Million square meters of green spaces. This thriving community offers residents a convenient and comfortable lifestyle. its unique design encompasses two interconnected compounds, Wesal Views & Wesal Residences, seamlessly linked by a large commercial spine. currently home to hundreds of families, Wesal boasts a comprehensive range of amenities and services, catering to all your needs. Experience the comfort of a dedicated commercial area, nurseries for your little ones, sports clubs to stay active, modern clinics for your healthcare needs, and ample green spaces to relax and unwind. Embracing diversity, Wesal also provides houses of worship, ensuring a harmonious community for all. In collaboration with kharafi national, Wesal offers comprehensive facility management services. From maintenance and security to promoting environmental awareness, we prioritize your safety, peace of mind, and well-being. Wesal offers a hassle-free choice for homeowners; take advantage of our flexible payment plans, requiring only a 10% down payment. Your dream home awaits in Wesal, where comfort, functionality, and community converge.
